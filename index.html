About Us

Founded in 1986 as Trinity Engineering, Inc., Trinity | ERD is the brainchild of Colin Murphy, who wanted to start a company that would allow him to apply his wealth of experience providing solutions for analyzing, improving and developing the building envelope. Our headquarters in Seattle, Washington are where the majority of our architects, engineers, project managers and administrative support staff are housed. Our accredited laboratory can perform scores of different tests to be sure materials meet code expectations, windows do not fail before their rating, rooves meets warranty standards, and many other aspects of building envelope science. In 2010 we added an office in Honolulu, Hawaii to better serve our clients throughout the Pacific. Trinity | ERD continually administers projects located throughout the country and worldwide.

Our Services

Analysis

We bring a wide array of diagnostic and reporting tools to projects requiring detailed forensic analysis. We have an extensive database of roofing data and research covering thirty years of waterproofing technology, which allows us to mine historical data on products, systems, weather and other information that could be critical to your buildingâ€™s health, at a moments notice.

Design

At the heart of a watertight, long-lived building envelope lies good design. Our engineers and architects work to create a system that performs best based on local weather, building conditions and the overall purpose of the structure. We base design decisions on broad product knowledge, an extensive library of product and historical data, and sound design and engineering principles.

Litigation Support

We have the experience and the credentials necessary to provide forensic studies, expert testimony and litigation support for building envelope and construction related disputes. Our team works diligently to provide compelling, unbiased, and accurate reports. In any dispute our goal is to enable your advocates to work toward a resolution backed by solid, well-presented information.

Contract Administration

We have a well-developed process for contract support that promotes good decision making at all project phases. From the initial request for a bid to its installation, we make sure that your project is executed according to industry standards. We literally wrote the book on contract support; Colin Murphy's Roofing for General Contractors is a complete guide.
