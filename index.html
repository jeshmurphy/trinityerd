About Us

Founded in 1986 as Trinity Engineering, Inc., Colin Murphy wanted to form a company that would allow him to apply his wealth of experience providing solutions for analyzing, improving and developing the building envelope. The name "Trinity" came from a group of companies then owned by Mr. Murphy that were all named after the Trinity River in northern California, where he fished while working in the state in the late 1970's. He describes it as an “almost religious” experience: "It was one of the most beautiful places I had ever fished,” says Mr. Murphy. “I had an old fiberglass fly rod that I still have today as a memory of the times." Through the years, the company expanded throughout the country, but the name, "Trinity Engineering" could not be used in all states. Looking for a name that described who we are, and the breadth of services we could provide, the name Exterior Research & Design (ERD) was born. Since we have been in business for twenty years, many people still identify us as “Trinity,” especially the firms we worked with internationally. As we marked the twentieth anniversary of the firm in 2006, we combined our heritage with our present, to become Trinity | ERD. Trinity | ERD not represents half a lifetime's work and a proud history. Today, the company includes over 40 staff members, a full-service testing facility, and clients from across North America, the Middle East, and Europe. We provide numerous services related to the building envelope and oversee a diverse set of building envelope projects throughout the world.

Our Services

Analysis

Accurate assessment of building envelope and waterproofing issues are essential to TKTKT and Trinity | ERD brings a wide array of diagnostic and reporting tools to projects requiring detailed forensic analysis. We have an extensive database of roofing data and research covering thirty years of waterproofing technology, which allows us to mine historical data on products, systems, weather and other information that could be critical to your building’s health, at a moment's notice.

Design

Our engineers and architects work together to create a system that performs best based on local weather, building conditions and the overall purpose of the structure. We base design decisions on broad product knowledge, an extensive library of product and historical data, and sound design and engineering principles.

Litigation Support

Building envelope and construction problems sometimes need to be resolved through litigation or arbitration. When that happens, you need to be armed with information presented in a way that makes as much sense to attorneys as it does to engineers.

Contract Administration

Trinity | ERD supervises roofing and waterproofing installations on structures around the world. From the initial request for a bid to its installation, we can help you make sure that your project is executed well and according to industry standards.
