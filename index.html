About Us

As a firm, Trinity | ERD brings over 30 years of waterproofing expertise to confront the challenges with building envelope design in residential, commercial and mixed use facilities. Trinity | ERD was founded by Colin Murphy in 1986, and he remains the principal of this firm to this day. Our continued strength as a firm lies in our systematic approach to addressing the challenges with waterproofing designs using materials and innovation that will maintain a structure’s aesthetic elements and function while protecting the building from water intrusion. We strive to adapt to changes in the construction market by continuously evaluating new products and incorporating those products with proven, long-term performance into our design and specifications.

Our Team

Trinity | ERD’s project teams are selected based on their expertise and experience. Our staff include project managers, field inspectors, architects, drafters, and administrative personnel. Trinity | ERD is committed to supplying sufficient manpower and capacity to easily accomplish the tasks included in the projected schedule.
