History

Founded in 1986 as Trinity Engineering, Inc., Colin Murphy wanted to form a company that would allow him to apply his wealth of experience providing solutions for analyzing, improving and developing the building envelope.

The name "Trinity" came from a group of companies then owned by Mr. Murphy that were all named after the Trinity River in northern California, where he fished while working in the state in the late 1970's. He describes it as an “almost religious” experience: "It was one of the most beautiful places I had ever fished,” says Mr. Murphy. “I had an old fiberglass fly rod that I still have today as a memory of the times."

Through the years, the company expanded throughout the country, but the name, "Trinity Engineering" could not be used in all states. Looking for a name that described who we are, and the breadth of services we could provide, the name Exterior Research & Design (ERD) was born.

Since we have been in business for twenty years, many people still identify us as “Trinity,” especially the firms we worked with internationally. As we marked the twentieth anniversary of the firm in 2006, we combined our heritage with our present, to become Trinity | ERD.

Trinity | ERD not represents half a lifetime's work and a proud history. Today, the company includes over 40 staff members, a full-service testing facility, and clients from across North America, the Middle East, and Europe. We provide numerous services related to the building envelope and oversee a diverse set of building envelope projects throughout the world.
